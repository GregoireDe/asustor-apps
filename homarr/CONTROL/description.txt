A simple, yet powerful dashboard for your server.
Simplify the management of your server with Homarr - a sleek, modern dashboard that puts all of your apps and services at your fingertips.
Homarr seamlessly integrates with the apps you've added, providing you with valuable information and giving you complete control. Installation is a breeze, and Homarr supports a wide range of deployment methods.

Note:
1. Version 1.X introduce a major breaking change, please follow official documentation and save your previous configuration (0.15) before installation: https://homarr.dev/blog/2024/09/23/version-1.0/
2. The docker version is maintained by the developer.
